Bike Trip Analysis in New York City from July-December 2017

The embedded Tableau dashboard below provides insight into bike trips recorded in NYC from July through December 2017.
You can click on individual tabs at the top of the dashboard to find out more details that occurred within this particular timespan.


Here are some examples of the interesting facts found during the analysis that can be seen in the various dashboard tabs below:

  • Total Number of Trips Recorded: 9.548 million
  • Peak Time to Start a Trip in Both Summer and Winter Months: 5 pm
  • Gender Breakdown of Riders: 6.3 million Male compared to 2.2 million Female
  • Average Distance that a Citi Bike was Ridden during this timespan: 1,017 miles

    • To discover more enlightening info, interact with the dashboard below especially the Bike Station Usage map that changes when you select a different month. You will see the most/least popular stations each month and also find out that there are more riders in the summer months while the number of riders drastically decreases in the winter months as expected when the cold weather impacts New York City travel.
